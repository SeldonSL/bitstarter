Eidetic memory, commonly referred to as photographic memory or total recall, is the ability to recall images, sounds or objects in memory with great precision (Wikipedia)

In the near future, wearable display devices, such as Google Glass, will become ubiquitous. You will be able to access information about things you are seeing, ask for directions, be reminded of important appointments as you walk and take pictures and record video whenever you want.

But... what about questions like: when did I met this person?, what was the address of the event I saw last night on TV? and the always important... where did I leave the car keys? Here comes Eidetik!

Eidetik will harness the powerful processing power of the cloud, the most advanced pattern recognition algorithms and all the data that wearable and mobile devices offer to build an indexed database of your daily experiences. Think of a personalized Google search of your memories!

When active, Eidetik will be continously taking snapshots of your device camera, registering audio logs, reading location and other type of information available, sending it to the cloud for offline processing. Context awareness can be introduced if other devices are present like smartphones and computers in order to incorporate information like which emails were you reading or what videogame were you playing.

In the beginning, Eidetik will be able to index some important image patterns like faces and words (OCR), translate audio to speech, incorporate location and time awareness and also some user-defined tag information. But the future is limitless!
